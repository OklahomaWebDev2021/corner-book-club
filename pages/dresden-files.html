The Dresden Files

The Harry Dresden Files series of books is my favorite book series. I was intransed the first time that I read Storm Front. I could not wait for each new book to come out. I was deeply saddened by the end of Battlegrounds, but cannot wait to see where Harry's life goes from here.

Harry has always had a very good moral compass that many of the characters question throughout the books. One exception is his best friend Michael Carpenter.

Harry has been dogged by the White Council of Wizards because of what he was to become. It is hard not to wonder if that purpose is still going on throughout the books even with the death of the main person attempting to shape Harry against his moral compass.

Book Series Facts:

Author Jim Butcher
Genre Fantasy and Detective
Rating Mature
Audio Reader: James Marsters
Perspective From Harry's point of view
Things of Note These are things to consider when thinking about reading this series:
  • As books progress, so does the swearing (esp. The Law)
  • Sex or sexual references are pretty much throughout the books (esp. Blood Rites)
  • Death is throughout the books
What Makes Me Read

In this series, I dont read them for the sex or cursing or even the death. I read them because I like the way Jim Butcher develops each of the characters and the relationships between them and how they deal with moral dillema and the whole right and wrong of it all.

I like how Jim Butcher brings about every creature of fairy and monster lore into his stories. I like how there is almost if not always a moral right the characters try to adhere to as the stories progress. There is also consequence to most of the decisions that I can find whether the decision is good or bad.

Finally, I like his ability to be able to insert other stories into the series that give more insight into the characters and events of the series. One thing about the stories that I am wondering if Jim Butcher will address, and I am sure he will at some point, is the curse placed on Harry to "die alone". Every woman he has loved has died around him except for Elane.

Side Jobs

  • Restoration of Faith (takes place before Storm Front)
  • Vignette (takes place between Death Masks and Blood Rites)
  • Something Borrowed (takes place between Dead Beat and Proven Guilty
  • It's My Birthday Too (takes place between White Night and Small Favor
  • Heorot (takes place between White Night and Small Favor)

  • Backup (takes place between Small Favor and Turn Coat)
  • The Warrior (takes place between Small Favor and Turn Coat)
  • Last Call (takes place between Small Favor and Turn Coat)

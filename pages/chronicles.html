The Chronicles of Light and Shadow

The Chronicles of Light and Shadow are about Elle Chance, who is born into a rich family in England. She owns and runs her own dirigible and hauls freight to and from locations. Her mother has passed and she lives with here eccentric father. One of her fares lands her in a heap of trouble and brings up the past. Her, her docking agent, brings her into contact with a Mr. Marsh and her whirlwind adventure begins. Now she must stop the coming alchemical apocolypse.

Sky Pirates

Harry is beating himself up after his girlfriend, Susan, has been half turned by the red court. He meets Mab, the queen of the winter faries. She gets him to work for her, against his better judgment, to find a killer and the thief of the Summer Knights mantle. In the end he's up going up against a fairy queen in a battle that encompasses the entire of winter.
